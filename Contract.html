Such Beauty Services shall be provided at the Location and Time specified by such User, agreed to by Stylist, and at the Pricing quoted by Stylist or App. The booking of such Beauty Services shall be deemed an “Appointment” or “Booking” for purposes of this Agreement.

Stylist agrees to list on Stylist’s Profile accurate and up to date Pricing information for Beauty Services. Stylist’s Pricing can be transmitted by any means approved by Home Cuts.
